ABOUT

AIXDATA is the CSULB AI Club's annual datathon — a one-day competition where teams extract insights from real-world datasets and present their findings to a panel of judges. Open to all CSULB students with no experience required.

Whether you're running your first regression or fine-tuning a neural net, there's a track for you. Workshops, mentors, free food, and prizes all day.

Tracks

Three distinct competition tracks. Pick your focus — or go broad.

Click any card to flip it.

SUPERVISED

Supervised Learning

Work with labeled datasets to build classification and regression models. Predict outcomes, score accuracy, and optimize performance.

  • Classification & regression
  • Feature engineering
  • Model evaluation
UNSUPERVISED

Unsupervised Learning

Discover hidden patterns in unlabeled data. Cluster, compress, and explore without predefined answers.

  • Clustering (K-Means, DBSCAN)
  • Dimensionality reduction
  • Anomaly detection
COMPUTER
VISION

Computer Vision

Build models that see. Classify images, detect objects, and extract meaning from visual data.

  • Object detection
  • CNNs & transfer learning
